Project Overview

Overview

Application Description and Purpose:

The web application will serve as a platform to promote and provide information about a potential dentistry practice for my friend, Lawson Rhodes. The primary purpose is to attract new patients, provide existing patients with relevant information, and establish an online presence for the dental practice.

Intended Users:

The website targets individuals seeking dental services in the local area, including families, professionals, and individuals with specific dental needs. It aims to appeal to both existing patients and potential new clients.

Content:

The website will include details about the dental services offered, information about the dentists and staff, testimonials from satisfied patients, appointment scheduling options, dental care tips, and contact information.

  2. Services Page
    • Purpose: To provide detailed information about the dental services offered
    • Audience: Individuals interested in learning about available treatments
    • Content: List of services offered, descriptions, and any specializations

    • Purpose: To introduce the dentists and staff members
    • Audience: Patients curious about the professionals behind the practice

    • Interactive Elements: Staff bios
    • Actions: N/A
  4. Testimonials Page
    • Purpose: Showcase testimonials from satisfied patients
    • Audience: Visitors interested in hearing about others' experiences with the practice
    • Content: Collection of testimonials, including quotes, names, and possibly photos of patients (with consent)
    • Data Entry: No
    • Validations: In the footer
    • Interactive Elements: Testimonial listings in a carousel
    • Actions: N/A
  5. Appointments Page
    • Purpose: To facilitate appointment scheduling for patients
    • Audience: Existing patients and individuals seeking dental care
    • Content: Appointment booking form, availability calendar, contact information
    • Data Entry: Form for booking appointments
    • Validations: Required fields, date and time selection, HTML/CSS in the footer
    • Interactive Elements: Appointment form, calendar
    • Actions: Submitting the form will schedule an appointment
  6. Contact Page
    • Purpose: To provide contact information and location details
    • Audience: Individuals wishing to get in touch with the dental practice
    • Content: Contact form, address, phone number, map
    • Data Entry: Yes, for contacting the practice
    • Validations: Required fields in contact form, HTML/CSS in the footer
    • Interactive Elements: Contact form, embedded map
    • Actions: Submitting the form will send a message to the practice
